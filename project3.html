Responsive Design Importance

Responsive design is crucial as it ensures that your website adapts and displays correctly on various devices and screen sizes. This enhances user experience, increases engagement, and boosts conversion rates, as visitors can easily access your content regardless of the device they're using.

Google Analytics Reports

Google Analytics provides valuable insights into user behavior, including audience, acquisition, and behavior reports. These reports can reveal what content your target audience is accessing, how they're interacting with your site, and where they're coming from, allowing you to make informed decisions to improve your website's performance.
